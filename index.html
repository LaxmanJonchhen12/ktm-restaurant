About Us

Kathmandu Restaurant is one of the finest Nepalese restaurant located at Des Moines, IA.

Our restaurant is best known for its outstanding Nepalese Cuisine, excellent staff service with livery ambience. Kathmandu Restaurant prepares everything fresh using the best quality ingredients and the freshest vegetables. Each dish represents flavor, pleasure, affection and essence.

At Kathmandu Restaurant we blend taste and emotion into a one of a kind delightful experience. Our restaurant offers Lunch buffet, dinner, takeout for our valuable customers.
